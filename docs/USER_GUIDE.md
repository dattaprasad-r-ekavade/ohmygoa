## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Register** in the top navigation
2. Choose your account type:
   - **Free User** - Browse and purchase coupons
   - **Business User** - Create listings and manage business (‚Çπ499/month)
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Phone number
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile

### Logging In

1. Click **Login** in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

### Forgot Password

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Click the reset link and create a new password

## Free User Features

### Browsing Listings

1. Use the search bar to find businesses, events, jobs, or products
2. Apply filters:
   - Category
   - Location
   - Price range
   - Rating
   - Features (e.g., parking, WiFi)
3. Click on any listing to view details

### Searching

**Quick Search:**
- Use the search bar on any page
- Enter keywords (e.g., "beach resort Calangute")
- Press Enter or click Search

**Advanced Search:**
- Click **Advanced Search** below the search bar
- Select specific categories, locations, and filters
- Set price range and other criteria
- Click **Search** to see results

**Save Searches:**
- After searching, click **Save This Search**
- Get notified when new matching listings are added
- View saved searches in your dashboard

### Viewing Business Details

Listing pages include:
- Photos and videos
- Description and features
- Contact information
- Location map
- Opening hours
- Price range
- Customer reviews
- Related listings

### Bookmarking Favorites

1. Click the heart icon ‚ù§Ô∏è on any listing
2. View all your bookmarks in **Dashboard ‚Üí Saved Listings**
3. Remove bookmarks by clicking the heart again

### Leaving Reviews

1. Navigate to a business listing you've visited
2. Click **Write a Review**
3. Rate the business (1-5 stars)
4. Write your review (minimum 50 characters)
5. Add photos (optional)
6. Click **Submit Review**

**Review Guidelines:**
- Be honest and constructive
- Focus on your personal experience
- Include specific details
- Avoid offensive language
- One review per business per user

### Purchasing Coupons

1. Browse available coupons or find them on business listings
2. Click **View Coupon** or **Get Deal**
3. Review coupon details:
   - Discount amount
   - Validity period
   - Terms and conditions
4. Click **Purchase Coupon**
5. Complete payment
6. Receive coupon code via email and dashboard
7. Show coupon code to the business to redeem

**Note:** 10% commission is automatically deducted from all coupon sales and goes to the platform.

### Submitting Enquiries

1. On any listing page, click **Send Enquiry**
2. Fill in the enquiry form:
   - Your name and contact details
   - Message/questions
   - Preferred contact method
3. Click **Send Enquiry**
4. The business owner will receive your enquiry and contact you directly

### Managing Notifications

**View Notifications:**
- Click the bell icon üîî in the top navigation

- Click to mark as read

**Notification Settings:**
- Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Notifications**

   - Email address

   - Upload photos (max 10)

7. **Step 5: Additional Details**
   - Opening hours
   - Price range
   - Amenities/features
   - Payment methods accepted

   - Meta description
   - Keywords

9. Click **Submit for Review**

**Note:** All listings are reviewed by admins before going live (usually within 24 hours).

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is registration free?**
A: Yes, registration is completely free for browsing and purchasing coupons.

**Q: Do I need a subscription to browse?**
A: No, you can browse all listings without a subscription. Subscription is only needed to create business listings.

**Q: How do I contact a business?**
A: Click on the listing and use the "Send Enquiry" form or call/email directly using the provided contact information.

### Business User Questions

**Q: How long does listing approval take?**
A: Most listings are reviewed and approved within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime from Dashboard ‚Üí Subscription. Access continues until the end of the billing period.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets via Razorpay.

**Q: How often can I request payouts?**
A: You can request payouts anytime your balance exceeds ‚Çπ1000. Payouts are processed within 3-5 business days.

**Q: Can I have multiple listings?**
A: Yes, Premium subscribers can create unlimited listings.

### Coupon Questions

**Q: How do I redeem a coupon?**
A: Show the coupon code (from your email or dashboard) to the business at the time of purchase or booking.

**Q: Can I get a refund on coupons?**
A: Refund policies vary by business. Check the terms and conditions before purchasing.

**Q: Do coupons expire?**
A: Yes, each coupon has a validity period. Check the expiry date before purchasing.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Mobile apps are coming soon. The website is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Why can't I see my listing?**
A: New listings require admin approval. Check your email for approval status.
