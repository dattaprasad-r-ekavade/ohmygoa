# Ohmygoa — App Concept (MVP Version)

## Overview

Ohmygoa is a comprehensive directory and community platform designed exclusively for Goa, India. It helps users easily discover local businesses, restaurants, services, attractions, events, jobs, and more. The platform offers detailed listings, user reviews, ratings, access to free or paid coupons, community features, and local content including news, blogs, and classifieds.

---

## User Types

### 1. Free Users

Free users can:

- Browse all business listings, services, and attractions
- View ratings, reviews, photos, and basic details
- Access available free coupons
- Purchase premium coupons offered by businesses
- Explore local events, workshops, and happenings in Goa
- Browse job listings and apply for local employment opportunities
- Read community blogs, articles, and travel guides about Goa
- Access local news and updates
- Browse the classifieds section for buying/selling items, property rentals, and services
- Discover service experts (photographers, tour guides, local experts)
- Shop from local product marketplace (souvenirs, handicrafts, Goan products)
- Explore Goa's tourist places, beaches, monuments, and attractions
- Participate in community discussions and Q&A forums

### 2. Business Users

Business owners can:

- Create and manage their verified business profile
- Update information, photos, menus, and offers
- Respond to customer reviews
- Access basic analytics such as views and user engagement
- Post and manage events (workshops, promotions, special occasions)
- List job openings and manage applications
- Create and publish blog posts to share stories and insights
- Sell products through the integrated marketplace
- Post service offerings in the Service Expert directory
- Submit news and announcements about their business
- Create classified ads for services or products
- List their location on the Places directory (for tourist attractions, resorts, etc.)

#### Subscription Plan (MVP):

**Premium Plan – ₹499/month**

Business users get:

- Advanced profile customization
- Priority placement in search results across all sections (listings, events, jobs, products)
- Detailed analytics & insights for business, events, and product performance
- Ability to offer exclusive coupons to users
- Featured placement in Events, Jobs, and Product sections
- Enhanced blog post visibility and SEO benefits
- Ability to post unlimited classified ads
- Priority support and faster verification

### 3. Admin Users

Admin users have complete control over:

- User account management (free, premium, business users)
- Business verification and claim approvals
- Content moderation across all sections:
  - Business listings
  - Events
  - Jobs postings
  - Blog posts and articles
  - News submissions
  - Classifieds ads
  - Product listings
  - Service expert profiles
  - Community posts and comments
  - Places/attractions listings
- Viewing and managing app-wide analytics:
  - User engagement metrics
  - Business performance
  - Event attendance tracking
  - Job application statistics
  - Blog/news readership
  - Product sales data
  - Revenue and commission reports
- Approving and managing ad spaces & coupon listings
- Managing featured content across all sections
- Category and tag management for all content types
- SEO and meta tag management
- Newsletter and email campaign management

**Commission:** Admins receive 10% commission on all coupons sold through the app.

## Ad Spaces for Businesses

Businesses can purchase advertising slots on the platform. These include:

- Homepage banner ads
- Category page ads (for business listings, events, jobs, products, etc.)
- Search results page placements
- Event page sidebar ads
- Job listing page ads
- Blog/News article sidebar ads
- Product marketplace banner ads
- Classifieds section ads

Ads may include images, text, and direct links to the business's Ohmygoa listing or external website. Rates are set and managed by Admin.

---

## Payment & Financial Flow

### Subscription & Ad Purchases

Payments from business users for subscriptions and ad spaces are processed securely through **Razorpay**, integrated within the app.

### Coupon Sales

Users can purchase coupons offered by businesses through the app.

- Revenue is split automatically — business receives its amount, and Admin receives a 10% commission.

### Business Payouts

Businesses can redeem their coupon earnings once they reach a minimum balance of **₹1000**.

- Payments are made via bank transfer to the business's registered bank account within 5–7 business days.

## Additional Platform Features
